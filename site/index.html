Is Product Coaching For Me?

I am dedicated to solving the unique challenges faced by early- to mid-career product managers in the bioscience, biotech, and healthcare industries.

Navigating the complexities of product roadmaps, prioritizing and managing product features, effectively communicating with stakeholders, and conducting user research with a scientific audience are just a few of the issues I address. By providing personalized guidance and support, I work closely with clients to help them overcome obstacles, drive successful product launches, and create impactful solutions for their target audience.

Additionally, I help you create actionable plans to achieve your career goals, whether that's learning a specific skillset, or getting the next promotion.

About Me

After devoting a decade of my career to improving healthcare and patient outcomes, I left my last job at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard to start this product coaching business.

My qualifications as a strong product manager, combined with my passion for empowering others, make me the perfect partner for those seeking to enhance their skills and reach new heights in these rapidly evolving industries. Committed to helping you unlock your full potential and excel in your career, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to help clients excel in their roles and create innovative solutions that make a meaningful impact on human health outcomes.

Why Work With Me?

Choosing to work with me is a decision to invest in your future success. I genuinely care about your growth in the bioscience, biotech, and healthcare industries and understand the specific challenges you face.

By tailoring my coaching to address your individual needs and goals, I can provide the support and guidance necessary to elevate your skills and achieve lasting results. My proven track record of guiding clients towards success, coupled with my passion for empowering others, makes me the ideal choice for those seeking to make a meaningful impact within these industries.
